The American Cops Officers Alliance will focus part of the next year on promoting issues associating with civilian authorities oversight boards..

Civilian oversight boards are frequently drifted as a popular idea after news breaks of police misbehavior or an officer included shooting. These oversight boards have actually been long criticized by both the political left and right for inconsistent discipline, spending plan problems and selecting members at the desires of the politically linked..

Political Nonprofit That Supports Police

" As more towns think about civilian oversight boards to discipline authorities misbehavior, there have been various concerns that have actually plagued the efficiency of these boards," said Dan Stuebs, Executive Director of the American Authorities Officers Alliance. "The American Cops Officers Alliance is devoted to engaging the general public and the citizens on the issues that currently plague oversight boards already in place. From irregular discipline of officers in New York City to excessive spending plans in Chicago, we are committed to informing the concerns relating to these boards and oppose any future efforts that possibly objected to by ballot procedures in 2019.".
