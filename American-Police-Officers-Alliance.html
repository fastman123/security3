Who Is American Police Officers Alliance

The American Cops Officers Alliance will focus part of the next year on advocating problems connecting to civilian authorities oversight boards..

Civilian oversight boards are typically floated as a popular idea after news breaks of cops misconduct or an officer included shooting. These oversight boards have actually been long criticized by both the political left and right for inconsistent discipline, budget plan issues and selecting members at the desires of the politically linked..

Political Nonprofit That Supports Police

" As more towns consider civilian oversight boards to discipline police misbehavior, there have actually been numerous problems that have actually plagued the effectiveness of these boards," stated Dan Stuebs, Executive Director of the American Cops Officers Alliance. "The American Cops Officers Alliance is committed to engaging the general public and the citizens on the problems that currently afflict oversight boards currently in location. From irregular discipline of officers in New York City to extreme spending plans in Chicago, we are dedicated to informing the concerns associating with these boards and oppose any future efforts that maybe objected to by ballot measures in 2019.".
