How does Invisalign work?
Invisalign uses sophisticated 3-D computer-imaging technology to figure out a complete treatment strategy from the preliminary position of your teeth to the last desired position. Then, clear aligners are custom-made for your teeth based on these results. Each is work for a couple weeks, and after that replaced by the next in the series, so your teeth will gradually relocate to the last ideal position.

What are the main advantages of Invisalign?
The Invisalign aligners are nearly clear, so it's likely people won't even observe you're wearing braces. And, unlike braces, you can consume all of the foods you delight in, and brush and floss as you normally would merely by eliminating the aligners. Lastly, Invisalign does not use any metal brackets or wires that might irritate your mouth - this likewise indicates you'll invest less time in the dental practitioner's chair due to the fact that you will not need to come in for changes.

Can I go to any dentist for treatment with Invisalign?
Dental practitioners who provide patients with Invisalign are required to undergo instructional sessions to become a trained Invisalign supplier. In addition, these dental practitioners should take part in ongoing training classes that will acquire them the designation of "Preferred Service provider." When picking the right dentist for your treatment, ask if they're a favored provider, or if they have actually been correctly trained to treat you with Invisalign.

Is Invisalign unpleasant?
Invisalign is able to correct your teeth without the discomfort related to the wires used in braces. However, it's possible for you to experience a momentary discomfort or pressure during the first couple of days of each aligner. This is all right and normal - it simply indicates the treatment is working and your teeth are getting closer to their last location.

Exist particular people that Invisalign isn't appropriate for?
Invisalign is suitable for many teens and adults. The only individuals that Invisalign isn't ideal for are those who still have baby teeth or second molars that have not appeared. If you think you're a prospect, your dental practitioner or orthodontist will have the ability to figure out if Invisalign treatment is right for you.

Are there foods I should avoid while utilizing Invisalign aligners?
No. Considering that the aligners can be removed, you have much more versatility in what you can consume or drink. The two main things that should be prevented while using Invisalign aligners is chewing gum and cigarette smoking, as it can discolor the aligners.
